About MYAH

MYAH Atelier is the creative expression of artisan Maria Ysabel, who transforms precious materials into wearable art. Each piece is meticulously crafted with attention to detail, balance, and harmony.

Our philosophy is rooted in the belief that jewelry should be an extension of one's personality—subtle yet expressive, delicate yet enduring. We draw inspiration from organic forms, architectural lines, and the quiet beauty of nature.

Every creation at MYAH tells a story, capturing moments of inspiration and translating them into pieces that resonate with emotion and meaning.

Creative Process

At MYAH, creation begins with inspiration drawn from the natural world—the curve of a leaf, the texture of stone, the play of light on water.

We carefully select materials that speak to our aesthetic: recycled silver, ethically sourced gemstones, and unique elements that carry their own history.

Each piece undergoes a meticulous process of sketching, modeling, and handcrafting, ensuring that the final creation maintains the essence of the original inspiration while becoming a wearable work of art.

Our approach balances traditional techniques with contemporary design, resulting in jewelry that feels both timeless and modern.
